## üéì Methodology Validation

### Why These Metrics?

**Detached House %:** Strong predictor of subdivision potential (can't subdivide apartments)
**Age 55+:** Peak downsizing age when kids leave home, retirement nears
**Income:** Determines ability to afford new housing and quality of developments
**Education:** Proxy for white-collar employment, lifestyle preferences, apartment acceptance
**Urban Proximity:** Determines development feasibility and market demand

### Score Calibration

Scores validated against known redevelopment hotspots:
- High-scoring areas match observed development activity
- Low-scoring areas match stable/rural/already-dense locations
- Medium-scoring areas align with "watch list" suburbs

### Time-Decay Model Basis

Exponential growth model chosen because:
- Housing transformation is cumulative (each development enables next)
- Network effects (amenities attract more development)
- Matches observed patterns in established redevelopment areas
- Conservative estimates (actual change may be faster with catalysts)
